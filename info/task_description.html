You most likely know the game where two players are in turn taking from a pile from 1 to 3 stones, and loses the one who took the last stone. We'll slightly generalize this task. Let's assume that both players can take not 1, 2, 3 stones, but k1, k2, …, km of stones. Here we are interested which player wins under the right circumstances (the game played properly).

Input: An amount of the rocks in the pile and the numbers you can take from it.

Output: Winner's number.
